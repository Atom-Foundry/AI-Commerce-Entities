# AI Commerce Entities

AI systems understand commerce through entities.

Entities represent products, brands, categories, attributes, reviews, merchants, and relationships.

## Core Commerce Entities

### Brand

A company, manufacturer, or merchant.

### Product

An item available for purchase.

### Category

A group of related products.

### Attribute

A characteristic of a product.

Examples:

- Size
- Color
- Material
- Weight

### Review

Customer-generated feedback used as a trust signal.

### Merchant

A store or marketplace selling products.

## Why Entities Matter

AI systems rely on entities and relationships to understand what products exist, who sells them, and when they should be recommended.

Entity quality directly impacts AI Understanding and Recommendation Visibility.
